IdeaForge

A WaForge Hackathon

Welcome to Idea Forge 2025, San Franciscos premier software hackathon. This event is all about bringing together innovators, developers, and problem-solvers to push the boundaries of whats possible with technology. Participants will design, build, and showcase impactful projects from AI-powered apps to tools that improve sustainability, education, and community engagement.

Our mission is to create a space where creativity meets code where students, hobbyists, and professionals can collaborate, learn, and prototype the next generation of software solutions.

Join us in San Francisco to code, brainstorm, and bring your ideas to life. No matter your skill level, youll find teammates, mentors, and resources to help forge your vision into reality.

About WAForge: WAForge is a hackathon founded by high schoolers. It provides a platform for young developers, to connect, learn, and showcase their talents. WAForge organizes events like Hackforges to foster innovation and help aspiring developers build impactful projects. WAForge also offers programs like Catalyst to help student developers take their projects to market, providing marketing, financial, and development aid. The ultimate vision is to be the premier platform for young talent across America, fostering a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts dedicated to making a difference through their ideas and creations.
